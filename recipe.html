Club Sandwich

Ingredients:
  • 3 slices of bread
  • 2 slices of tomato
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Grilled chicken or turkey slices
  • Mayonnaise
  • Bacon (optional)
How to Make:
  1. Toast the bread slices until golden brown.
  2. Spread mayo on each slice.
  3. Layer lettuce, tomato, and meat between slices.
  4. Secure with toothpicks and cut into quarters.

Sweet Potato Fries

Ingredients:
  • 2 sweet potatoes
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: paprika or garlic powder
How to Make:
  1. Peel and cut sweet potatoes into thin strips.
  2. Toss in olive oil, salt, and spices.
  3. Bake at 200°C (400°F) for 25–30 minutes.
  4. Serve crispy and hot with your favorite dip.
